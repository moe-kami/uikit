Who is Stella?

Stella, a captivating pop sensation, is fueled by the talents of its five members: Emma Miller, Olivia Clarke, Grace Anderson, Ava Taylor, and Sophia Smith. Each member brings a distinct flavor to the group, creating a harmonious blend of voices and personalities.

Drawing inspiration from the vibrant pop scene, Stella delivers a modern and engaging sound reminiscent of the genre's finest. With infectious melodies and heartfelt lyrics, their music resonates with a universal appeal.

Emma, Olivia, Grace, Ava, and Sophia unite to form a powerhouse of pop, promising audiences an unforgettable journey through the enchanting world of Stella.

Our journey

2017
Stella was formed

Stella was formed at a university in Sydney, Australia. Emma, Olivia, Grace, Ava, and Sophia was united by their shared love for music.

2018
Debut Single "Southern Lights"

Stella releases their debut single, "Southern Lights," captivating local audiences and setting the stage for their musical journey.

2020
International Breakthrough with "Harmony"

Stella achieves international acclaim with the release of their album "Harmony," featuring chart-topping hits like "Luminous Love" and "Galactic Dreams." Their music resonates with audiences worldwide, propelling them into the global spotlight.

2022
The first international tour

Stella's momentum continues to soar as they embark on their first international tour, captivating audiences across Europe, Asia, and North America.

2023
"Dreams of Southern Skies" Soars to the Top of the Charts

Stella makes a triumphant return with their highly anticipated album, "Dreams of the Southern Skies." Drawing inspiration from the breathtaking beauty of the Australian night sky and the mystical aura of the southern constellations, the album transports listeners to a realm of celestial enchantment.
